GROUP:      gateway.kgateway.dev
KIND:       GatewayExtension
VERSION:    v1alpha1

FIELD: grpcService <Object>


DESCRIPTION:
    GrpcService is the GRPC service that will handle the auth.
    Mutually exclusive with HttpService.
    
FIELDS:
  authority	<string>
    Authority is the authority header to use for the GRPC service.

  backendRef	<Object> -required-
    BackendRef references the backend GRPC service.

  requestTimeout	<string>
    RequestTimeout is the timeout for the gRPC request. This is the timeout for
    a specific request.

  retry	<Object>
    Retry specifies the retry policy for gRPC streams associated with the
    service.
